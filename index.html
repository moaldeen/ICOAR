Abstract

The tutorial discusses the increasing integration of Artificial Intelligence (AI) in daily life, focusing on its application to social cybersecurity issues like cyberharassment and cyberbullying. It introduces the Integrative Cyberinfrastructure for Online Abuse Research (ICOAR) platform, showcases research case studies on ICOAR's application, and presents Cyberbullying Labs for hands-on AI technology application against cyberbullying.

Prerequisites

  • No extensive knowledge or experience in technical areas is required for using the ICOAR platform.
  • Non-CS students do not need advanced knowledge in AI or cybersecurity.
  • CS students should have basic knowledge of Python programming and a basic understanding of machine learning concepts and model evaluation, though it's not mandatory.

Tutorial Schedule

The tutorial is designed to last 3 hours but can be condensed to 2 hours if there are time constraints.
